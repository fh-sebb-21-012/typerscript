About TyperScript data storage

TyperScript does not collect any data without asking and uses your browser's local storage to save your highscores. This means that your data stays on your machine no matter what.

Deleting TyperScript data

All your scores and data can be cleared by deleting the browsing data for this website in the browser settings.
